The Schrödinger Equation

Posted on 28/07/2023

The Schrödinger equation was formulated by Erwin Schrödinger in 1926, in order to address the phenomenon of quantization in quantum mechanics as an eigenvalue problem.

The Schrödinger equation is to Quantum Physics what Newton's 2nd law of motion is to Classical Mechanics - it serves as a starting point to predicting the future behaviour of a system as it evolves in time.
